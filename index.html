Our Mission

We exist, to the dismay of some, to the horror of others. We in the committee spread our ideas and values to those with attentive ears. Just as the entropy of the universe increases, so will we. We will not be put down like a mutt, we shall stand against those who dare to speak against our goals of uniting the world under our banner.
